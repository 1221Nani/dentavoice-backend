Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DentaVoice AI ("we", "us") collects, uses and protects personal data when you visit this website, try our voice demo, or use the DentaVoice AI receptionist service.

1. Who we are

DentaVoice AI is a voice AI receptionist service for dental clinics, operated by [LEGAL NAME / BUSINESS ENTITY], [REGISTERED ADDRESS]. Contact: hello@dentavoiceai.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Website visitors: basic technical data (browser type, approximate location, pages visited) via hosting and analytics providers.
  • Demo callers: if you call our demo line or use the browser voice demo, the audio of the conversation and any details you share (e.g. a name or phone number you mention) are processed in order to run the demo.
  • Contact form: name, email address, phone number, clinic name and any message you submit.
  • Clinic customers: account details, clinic configuration (services, hours, staff names) and call data handled on the clinic's behalf.

3. How patient call data is handled

When DentaVoice AI answers calls on behalf of a dental clinic, the clinic is the data controller for its patients' data, and we act as a data processor under Article 28 GDPR. We process call data only to deliver the service: answering calls, booking appointments, sending confirmations and producing call summaries for the clinic.

4. GDPR — legal bases and your rights

We process personal data on the following legal bases: performance of a contract (service delivery), legitimate interest (responding to enquiries, improving the service) and consent where required (e.g. marketing communications).

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase and port your personal data, to restrict or object to processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email hello@dentavoiceai.com. You may also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority — in Luxembourg, the CNPD (Commission nationale pour la protection des données).

6. Data retention

Demo call recordings and contact-form submissions are kept only as long as needed to respond and improve the demo, and no longer than 12 months. For clinic customers, call data retention periods are defined in the service agreement and controlled by the clinic.

7. Security

Calls and data transfers are encrypted in transit. Access to customer data is restricted and logged. We review our providers' security practices and certifications as part of vendor selection.

8. International transfers

Some providers process data outside the EU/EEA (for example in the United States). Where this occurs, transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses or an applicable adequacy decision (such as the EU–US Data Privacy Framework).

9. Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies and similar technologies required for the site and voice demo to function. We do not use advertising trackers.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version.
