How It Works

From first ring to confirmed booking
in under 2 minutes

Sophie handles the entire conversation — no hand-offs, no hold music, no double-bookings.

1

Patient calls your clinic number

Your existing Twilio number routes to Sophie. She answers in under 2 seconds, greets the caller warmly by your clinic name and asks how she can help.

⚡ Sub-2 second answer time
2

Sophie checks your live calendar

The patient asks about "next Tuesday." Sophie queries Google Calendar in real-time and offers up to 4 open time slots — only genuine availability, never invented times.

📅 Real-time Google Calendar sync
3

Appointment confirmed & logged instantly

Sophie collects name, phone and reason for the visit, reads it all back, creates the calendar event, logs it to Google Sheets and gives the patient a confirmation number — all in one call.

✅ Instant calendar event + Sheets log
4

You see everything in your existing tools

Every booking appears in your Google Calendar and Google Sheets immediately. No new software, no new login — works on any device your team already uses.
